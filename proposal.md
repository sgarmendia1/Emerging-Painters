## Client Brief

**Client:** My cousin Joseph is an art collector, and we both collect and are interested in very similar paintings since we buy paintings together. Here he will showcase the painters whose work he is currently buying and following.

**Purpose:** This website is meant to showcase painters he believes in, display selected artworks, and explain why he thinks their market can grow.

**Audience:** Visitors interested in art, collecting, and emerging painters. They need to quickly understand which artists he is focused on, see examples of the work, and understand his perspective on their value and potential.

**Key action:** The most important action is for visitors to explore the featured painters and understand my client’s point of view on why their markets may grow.

**Pages/sections needed:**

* Home
* Featured Painters
* Market View / Why These Artists
* Contact

**Featured artists:**

* Werner Bronkhorst
* Jack Penny
* Jacob Bakangu

**Content status:** My client has identified three painters he wants featured on the website: Werner Bronkhorst, Jack Penny, and Jacob Bakangu. The site will include images of their paintings and short explanations about why my cousin believes their markets have strong potential to grow. I still need final images and short written descriptions from my client before building the full site.

**Style preferences:** The site should feel clean, modern, and art-focused. It should use a minimal layout, readable typography, strong image presentation, and a professional tone. Likely colors are neutral tones such as black, white, gray, or cream.

**Inspiration sites:**

* Artist portfolio websites - clean layouts and strong image focus
* Gallery websites - professional presentation and easy navigation
* Contemporary art websites - minimal design that highlights artwork
